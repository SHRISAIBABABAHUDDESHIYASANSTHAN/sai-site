About US

Shree Saibaba was a Great Indian spiritual master, Guru and a Fakir. He always preached “SABKA MALIK EK” and “Shraddha aur Saburi”. The Saibaba always advocates / preached that all religions are same and one must regard all religions. Saibaba condemned the distinction based caste and religions among society. .
